PROJECT DOCUMENTATION - 2026

Deadline
or Die

A productivity platform engineered for individuals who have replaced healthy work habits with the primal fear of failure.

ROLE

Lead Developer &
Reluctant Project Manager

Timeline

3 Months (2026)

Deliverables

Web App, Dashboard,
& Notification System

Tools

React, Laravel,
& MySQL

THE PROBLEM

Modern productivity tools were too kind. Too forgiving. Too full of gentle nudges and pastel-colored encouragement that achieved absolutely nothing.

Users needed accountability — not a motivational quote and a confetti animation every time they completed a task that was already two weeks late.

"The app told me 'great job!' I had missed four deadlines." — User Feedback #3

THE SOLUTION

We stripped productivity down to its most honest form: a tracker that logs your tasks, timestamps your excuses, and presents your failure rate in a clean, beautiful dashboard you cannot look away from.

PROJECT DOCUMENTATION - 2025

Overqualified

A resume builder for people whose skills have significantly outpaced their current opportunities.

ROLE

Full Stack Developer

Timeline

2 Months (2025)

Deliverables

Web App, PDF Export,
& Template System

Tools

React, Python, HTML/CSS

THE PROBLEM

Existing resume builders were designed for the average candidate. Bland templates, limited formatting, and zero personality — tools built by people who had clearly never been rejected from a job they were overqualified for.

Applicants were dumbing down their experience just to fit inside a two-column grid and a 1MB file size limit.

"I copy-pasted my resume into their template and lost three years of my life." — User Interview #7

THE SOLUTION

Overqualified generates resumes so polished and precise that the document itself becomes an argument for hiring you. Smart layout logic, editorial typography, and a PDF export that doesn't look like it was printed in 2009.

PROJECT DOCUMENTATION - 2024

Do Not
Disturb

A focus timer for people who have read four books on productivity and still can't finish a single task. Blocks distractions, logs your sessions, and keeps a detailed record of exactly how little you accomplished.

ROLE

Frontend Developer & UX
Designer

Timeline

6 Weeks (2024)

Deliverables

Web App, Focus Dashboard
& Session Analytics

Tools

React, HTML/CSS/JS,
& Local Storage

THE PROBLEM

Focus apps were either too complicated to set up or too simple to be useful. Users spent more time customizing their productivity tool than actually being productive — which, in hindsight, was the whole problem.

Distraction had been gamified. Attention had been monetized. And the average session length before opening a new tab was eleven minutes.

"I downloaded four focus apps last month. I used none of them." — User Survey #2

THE SOLUTION

Do Not Disturb is a single-purpose, suit-wearing timer. No gamification. No streaks. No badges for doing the bare minimum. Just a session, a log, and a quiet, data-driven record of exactly how focused you were — or weren't.

PROJECT DOCUMENTATION - 2023

Perfectly
Fine

A minimalist mental wellness check-in platform for people who insist they are doing fine and have the data to prove it.

ROLE

Full Stack Developer

Timeline

3 Months (2023)

Deliverables

Web App, Mood Dashboard,
& Weekly Reports

Tools

React, Laravel, Python,
& MySQL

THE PROBLEM

Mental wellness apps were either overwhelming or infantilizing — either a 47-question onboarding form or an animated character asking if you remembered to drink water today.

Neither approach served the users who needed it most: the ones who were clearly not fine but had already committed to the bit.

"I'm fine. I just haven't slept properly since 2022." — Research Insight #1

THE SOLUTION

A clean, low-friction daily check-in that logs your mood, energy, and stress across three simple inputs. Weekly summaries surface patterns you hadn't noticed. No unsolicited advice. No push notifications at 8am. Just honest, beautiful data about the state of your own existence.
